About Us

Who are we and what are we trying to accomplish?

Founded in 2015 in a small San Jose State classroom by a team of four incredible "D's", also known as Web Ninjas, from San Jose State University, JobNinja provides an effectively innovative job search solution. The Web Ninjas come from a very diverse background, which they used to their advantage. As students Web Ninjas were faced with the eerie and ever so competitive ordeal of finding employment after graduation.Such diversity and ordeals paved way for Web Ninjas to to create an innovative and personal job search portal. A job portal that gives the end user a more personalized way to control their job search.
